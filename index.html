Profile Details

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor formula to estimate your baseline.

Your details calculate your baseline calorie burn. Your activity level applies a multiplier to this baseline to set your daily targets.

Gender Male and female formulas differ slightly due to metabolism differences.
Diet Goal We adjust your daily calorie target based on this: Maintain (no change) • Lose (–300 cal/day, ~0.2 kg/week) • Gain (+300 cal/day, ~0.2 kg/week).

Activity Level

Light (1.2×) • Moderate (1.55×) • Intense (1.725×) — each multiplies your baseline calorie burn.

Your activity level sets how many calories you burn daily. We use this to calculate your macro targets for the Daily Log.
